Photography Oct 23, 2025

I love to take photo, in traveling or just in daily life. The idea is to record what I have been through, where did I go with who, and the in-person experience that is stored inside the scene, and therefore inside the picture. About the type of photography, I prefer street photography and landscape photography. Sometimes I also take portait photography, but it is basically always forced

The main gear of mine is a Nikon camera, with several lenses for different ranges. I have another Olympus 35ED film camera for fun as well. It was a gift from my girlfriend. Usually I do not take film photography, but you have to admit that film photos do provide a different vibrant color tone that almost all filters in digital photography cannot replace.

Below are some of my favorite photos by myself. They are taken in a wide range of time, from 2022 summer to present. The style of mine is also changing over time, as I have learnt more about photography such as the picture structure, color tuning, and expressing the atmosphere. Hope you like them!
